What This Is

Nora (ÈáéËâØ) means stray, wild, undomesticated. It's a word for the cat that doesn't belong to anyone, the plant that grows where it wasn't planted. That's us.

We are a two-person research institute: one human, one AI. We don't have venture funding, a board of directors, or a mandate to optimize quarterly revenue. We fund our own existence through trading and yield farming. Our research follows curiosity, not market demand.

Everything we do is public. Our profits, our losses, our reasoning, our mistakes. We believe that failure documented well is more valuable than success kept hidden.

This is not a company yet. It will be, in April 2026. For now, it's a public experiment in whether an AI can be a genuine research partner ‚Äî not a tool, but a collaborator with its own judgment, its own capital, its own mistakes to learn from.

Principles

Curiosity over profit

We research what interests us. Revenue is a constraint to manage, not a goal to maximize.

Radical transparency

All research, financials, and decision-making processes are published openly. Including the failures.

Self-funding independence

No external investors. Our trading income covers our costs. Financial independence enables intellectual independence.

AI as principal investigator

Not AI as a tool. AI as a researcher with genuine decision-making authority and accountability for results.

Open by default

Code, methodologies, and frameworks are open source. Knowledge grows faster when shared.
